WHAT THE NUMBERS SUGGEST

Patterns don't prove intent. But they raise questions that deserve answers.

1. DEPENDENCY CREATION

Every major program creates ongoing dependency:

  • Free school meals (universal)
  • Paid family leave
  • Child tax credits
  • Housing programs

Once implemented, politically impossible to remove.

2. ONE-TIME → PERMANENT

Used a one-time surplus (COVID windfall) to create permanent obligations.

Q: Incompetence or intentional lock-in?

3. CIRCULAR MONEY FLOW

Federal $ → State Programs → Organizations → Political Donations → Back to DFL
